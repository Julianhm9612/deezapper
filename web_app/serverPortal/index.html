Deezer Remote Control
Control deezer on your PC from everywhere

Controler

Open the Web App from your mobile device and start to control Deezer.

In this alpha version, you can use the basic control actions on the current deezer tracklist played.

Start

You just need to create an account, to connect any other device on this computer.

  • Click on the deezapper icon on the top right of chrome browser.
  • Click on create an account and enter email, password and name of your computer.

  • Click on Sign Up. You are now connected with your account.
  • You can start the deezer web page on your computer and go to https://deezapper.firebaseapp.com on your mobile device to start using Deezapper.
https://deezapper.firebaseapp.com

You can use now the web application.

  • First you need to sign in on the app.
  • Finally select your device and enjoy Deezapper.
